About Fresh Tray

We're passionate about bringing fresh, healthy food directly to you. Our mobile food van travels throughout the city, serving nutritious meals made with the finest local ingredients.

Founded in 2025, Fresh Tray was born from the idea that healthy eating shouldn't be complicated or time-consuming. We believe everyone deserves access to delicious, wholesome food, wherever they are.
